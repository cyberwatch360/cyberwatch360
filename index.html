Executive Summary

Cyber Watch 360 revolutionizes incident management by integrating AI-driven automation with a unified operational platform, eliminating the inefficiencies of siloed tools. By consolidating alert triage, ticketing, task management, and threat intelligence enrichment into a single solution, CW360 empowers security teams to respond faster, reduce noise, and focus on high-impact threats.

The AI Assistant acts as a force multiplier—handling routine analysis, providing real-time recommendations, and serving as a virtual L1 analyst, ensuring 24/7 vigilance without overwhelming human operators.

The Problem

Alert Overload

Security teams are overwhelmed by a high volume of alerts generated from multiple disconnected platforms, leading to fatigue and missed threats.

Inefficient Prioritization

Traditional tools lack contextual intelligence, making it difficult to distinguish critical threats from low-risk alerts without automated risk scoring.

Zero-Day Threats

Signature-based detection systems fail to identify novel attack methods, leaving organizations vulnerable to zero-day exploits.

Fragmented Workflows

Disconnected ticketing platforms cause delays, with critical incidents often overlooked or mishandled due to inefficient workflows.
